Safety and quality

Real projects need real boundaries.

Some AV Science topics involve electricity, heat, pressure, or hazardous chemicals. The public guides are written as educational overviews unless a project is safe for beginners with ordinary materials. Advanced topics require qualified supervision, local rules, and proper equipment.
